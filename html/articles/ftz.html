Fight the Zeitgeist!

What is the Zeitgeist?
'Zeitgeist' is a German word mean 'The spirit of the age or time.' It's what's popular, what most people do.
So what does that make Fight the Zeitgeist? Fighting the Zeitgeist (FTZ) is, quite simply, being against stupid things that are popular. Yeah, that's a blanket statement, but blanket statements are always right. It's about all kinds of things:
  • FTZ is googling your name, and getting nothing.
  • FTZ is disliking the wearing of brands.
  • FTZ is wearing clothes because they are comfortable, without caring what they look like.
