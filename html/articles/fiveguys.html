How Many Menu Options Does Five Guys Have?

So the other day, I was at a Five Guys restaurant and, nerdy as I am, I got to thinking: how many different, individual things are there to order?
Well, let's start by looking at the menu:

We have:
  • 15 different toppings.
  • 8 different burgers.
  • 4 different sandwitches
  • 4 different hot dogs
  • 2 different types of fries
Let's start with the toppings: you can either have a topping, or you can't. Two choices. There are also 15 different menu toppings, this means that there are 215 different patterns of toppings to put on anything, which is 32768.
There are 8 different types of burgers to order, and each comes with toppings. We combine the two to get 8*215 different burgers to order, which is 262144.
Then, all we have to do is add 10, for the 4 sandwitches, 4 hot dogs, and 2 fries. This leaves us with a grand total of 262154 different menu items. Not bad for a menu scarcely bigger than my hand!
